WHAT DRIVES US

Let's face it, franchise establishments tend not to be where the most unique dishes are born. We locally source our ingredients, firmly believing in the mantra you get out what you put in.


We invite you to try what the Rebel Chef coined as International Fast Fusion, all your favorites from around the globe, fused with fresh, local and sustainable ingredients. We created something truly special for every palette in the Farmington, Minnesota area.
